Hi, I'm Yusun —
a designer who asks
why, a lot.

I'm a UX/UI designer based in Melbourne. I care deeply about how design shapes the way people experience the world — from the small friction of a confusing form to the larger questions of how technology fits into public life.

My work spans product design, service design, and design research. I tend to start with questions before reaching for solutions, and I believe the best design is often the kind people don't notice.

Previously I studied at Monash University, where I worked on projects ranging from healthcare apps to speculative research on robots in shared spaces.
