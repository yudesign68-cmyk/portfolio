Showcase · 2023

Dashboard
for Reading

A personal reading tracker dashboard that turns reading habits into visual stories — designed around the quiet luxury of having time to slow down and be present with a book.

Data Visualisation UI Design Responsive Design Visual Storytelling

Role

UI Designer

Team

Sole Project

Context

Academic Project

Output

High-fidelity UI Design

Background

Reinterpreting luxury as the quiet space to slow down.

Assigned the theme of "luxury," I chose not to interpret it through opulence or expense — but as something rarer: having the time and space to slow down. To feel calm, present, and at ease. Reading emerged as the perfect expression of this idea — a quiet indulgence, a moment of pause, a personal escape from the everyday.

The result is a thematic reading dashboard that captures timeless relaxation through a soft, elegant visual language — inspired by romantic novels and the warmth of a well-loved book. The interface integrates quantitative data (reading habits, progress tracking) with qualitative elements (book preferences, saved quotes), creating a customisable, immersive experience.

Design

A visual language built around calm, elegance, and personal habit.

Design Approach

Where data meets the feeling of a good book.

The dashboard balances two worlds: the precision of data visualisation and the warmth of a personal reading experience. Soft, muted tones reference aged paper and natural light. Typography leans into editorial elegance. Data is presented not as cold metrics, but as a reflection of personal habit and taste.

Responsive Design

Designed to adapt gracefully across screen sizes — from wide desktop dashboards to compact mobile views — without losing the warmth of the visual language.

Data Visualisation

Reading habits are translated into elegant, easy-to-read charts and progress trackers — making personal data feel meaningful rather than mechanical.

Visual Storytelling

Saved quotes, book covers, and reading milestones are woven into the layout — giving users a sense of their reading identity, not just their statistics.

Information Architecture

A clear hierarchy guides users from high-level stats to granular detail — making it easy to explore your reading life at whatever depth you want.
